The International Economics Olympiad

The International Economics Olympiad (IEO) is the most prestigious World Economics Competition for High School students and is held annually in a different country. The competition contains three subsections; Financial Literaly, Economics, and a Business Case.


To compete a student must be under 20 years of age and enrolled in a secondary school in Singapore. To be eligible for travel to the International competition the student must be at least 15 years of age at the time of travel.


In 2022, the Economics Olympiad will be held online from 26th July 2022 to 1st August 2022.
